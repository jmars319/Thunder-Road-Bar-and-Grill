About Us

Welcome to Thunder Road Bar & Grill, your local neighborhood spot in Midway, NC. We serve up delicious burgers, wings, sandwiches, and appetizers alongside cold drinks in a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're here for lunch, dinner, or to catch live music on the weekends, we've got you covered.

Join us for great food, friendly service, and good times. We're more than just a bar and grill—we're your neighborhood gathering place.
